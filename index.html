Atal Tinker Fest 2019

Be it history or looking ahead in technological advance, the love of science is something that should be embraced by all. It is very important for the students to explore and engage in enquiry based learning. Such exhibitions provide a platform whereby students indulge in hands on activities and project making. This surely nurtures their inquisitive minds and unleashes creativity.

World Scholar’s Cup

Learning Paths School, Mohali hosted the mega event ‘World Scholar’s Cup’, Chandigarh Round on the 26th and 27th of April, 2019. Upwards of 350 students participated from 27 Schools, across the tricity and beyond. The ceremony was initiated with the Vandana-Dance performance, by the students of LPS.

Book Launch of ‘The Write Path’- An Anthology of Short Stories

The Write Path – a short story collection written by the budding authors of the school was released by authors Lippi Parida, Chetna Keer, Preeti Parashar, publisher Hardeep Singh Chandpuri, the school principal Nutan Budhiraja and director Robin Aggarwal at Tagore Theatre, Sector 18, Chandigarh on August 9, 2019. A panel discussion highlighted the journey of the students as budding authors.
